# Hallucination Rate in GenAI Products

🔗 Live Demo: https://priyanshug09.github.io/PM-Portfolio/genai-product-metrics/hallucination-rate/

## 1. Concept

Hallucination rate is the percentage of AI-generated answers that are wrong, unsupported, fabricated, or misleading.

In simple words, hallucination happens when an AI gives an answer confidently, but the answer is not true or not grounded in the provided source.

## 2. Formula

Hallucination Rate = (Wrong or Unsupported AI Outputs / Total AI Outputs) × 100

## 3. Simple Example

Suppose we test an AI assistant with 100 questions.

Out of 100 answers:

- 75 answers are correct
- 10 answers are partially correct
- 15 answers are wrong, unsupported, or fabricated

So:

Hallucination Rate = 15 / 100 × 100 = 15%

This means the AI hallucinated in 15% of the tested cases.

## 4. Why This Metric Matters

GenAI products are not only judged by how fluent or fast the answer is.

They are judged by whether users can trust the answer.

A wrong AI answer can create serious problems in products such as:

- Healthcare assistants
- Legal assistants
- Customer support bots
- Finance chatbots
- HR policy assistants
- Enterprise knowledge assistants

## 5. Product Management Perspective

As an AI Product Manager or Business Analyst, I would track hallucination rate to measure the reliability of the AI system.

A useful AI answer should be:

- Correct
- Grounded in the available source
- Transparent about uncertainty
- Safe for the user
- Able to say “I don’t know” when the answer is not available
